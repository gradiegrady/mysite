Animate On Scroll

I have included an option to easily add animations to almost any element of your site content. These animations are triggered once elements enter the viewport of your browser as you scroll down the page adding life and energy to your site content. Great for adding focus to particular elements of your content which you wish to bring to the attention of your visitors.

Doing so is as simple as adding 2 class names to the element you wish to animate!

<div class="animate fadeIn">
   Animate this text!
</div>
